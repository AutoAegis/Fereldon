Data Privacy & Protection Policy

Your privacy and data security are extremely important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how it is used, and the rights you have regarding your personal information.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and password (encrypted).
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics such as login timestamps and site interactions.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your Ariselle account.
  • To maintain platform security, prevent abuse, and comply with safety requirements.
  • To improve website performance and user experience.
  • To provide customer support and respond to user inquiries.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored and handled using industry‑standard security practices:

  • All authentication data is protected using Firebase Authentication.
  • User information and activity logs stored in Firebase Firestore are encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • We implement additional safeguards such as rate-limiting, secure access rules, and continuous monitoring.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your data to third parties.

Your data is only shared in the following limited cases:

  • With trusted service providers that assist in operating the platform (e.g., Firebase), under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • If required to comply with legal obligations such as law enforcement requests.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Ariselle may use minimal, essential cookies for authentication and security purposes. We do not use advertising cookies or third‑party tracking cookies.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as your account remains active or as needed for legal and security purposes. Once an account is deleted, all associated personal data is permanently removed from our systems within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: You may request a copy of the data we store about you.
  • Correction: You may update or correct your account information at any time.
  • Deletion: You may request full account deletion, which permanently removes your data.
  • Security Concerns: You may contact us regarding any privacy or security issues.

8. Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy, you can reach us at:
support@ariselle.com
